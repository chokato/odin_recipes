The Soup Kitchen

In the Neopian Marketplace, there is a very large cauldron of soup! The Soup Faerie looks after it and makes sure there is always a plentiful supply for hungry pets.

Only really poor neopets (under 3,000 NP) can get handouts at the Soup Kitchen.
