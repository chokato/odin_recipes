Watercress Soup

Description

A cooling and nutritious earthy soup, watercress soup can help alleviate dry coughs, sore throats and irritability.

The health benefits of watercress include cancer prevention, lowering blood pressure, maintaining healthy bones and treating diabetes.

Recipe adapted from Souper Diaries

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 1 bunch of watercress — ends trimmed and washed
  • 300g pork ribs
  • 1 carrot — peeled and cut into chunks
  • 6-8 red dates
  • 2 honey dates
  • 3 tbsp goji berries
  • 6-8 dried oysters
  • 20g ikan bilis (dried anchovies) — rinsed
  • 6 cups water
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Blanch pork ribs in a small pot of boiling water for about 5 minutes.
  2. Put in all the ingredients in a soup pot. Add in enough water to submerge all the ingredients by 1 to 2 inches.
  3. Bring to a boil and let it simmer on the lowest heat for 1.5-2 hours or until the meat is soft.
  4. Season with salt to taste and serve hot.
