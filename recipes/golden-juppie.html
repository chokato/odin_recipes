Golden Juppie Soup

A shimmering delight fit for any Neopian adventurer, this hearty soup blends the sweetness of ripe Juppies with the warmth of golden spices. Perfect for chilly days in Faerieland or a cozy evening in your Neohome, every spoonful is a hug for your soul.

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe Golden Juppies, peeled and diced
  • 2 cups Faerie Spring Water
  • 1 cup fresh Creamberries
  • 1 large Carrotella, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Glowspice
  • 1 teaspoon Whirlwind Pepper
  • A sprinkle of Cloudfluff Salt
  • A pinch of Dusty Star Crystals

Steps

  1. In a shimmering pot, heat the Faerie Spring Water until it begins to dance with tiny bubbles.
  2. Add the chopped Carrotella and let it soften, stirring occasionally to release its sweetness.
  3. Toss in the Golden Juppies, Glowspice, Whirlwind Pepper, and Cloudfluff Salt.
  4. Simmer until the Juppies melt into a golden swirl.
  5. Gently stir in the Creamberries, letting the soup turn creamy and rich like sunlight in a bowl.
  6. Taste your magical creation and, if needed, sprinkle in Dusty Star Crystals for a hint of sweetness.
