Black Bean Soup

Description

This Chinese black bean soup is a popular soup for Chinese ladies in confinement and it is also suitable for men and children as well.

Black beans are not only delicious but they are prized for their rich source of protein, fibre and antioxidants.

Recipe adapted from Souper Diaries

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 150g black beans — soaked for an hour and discard soaking water
  • 300g pork ribs (or soft bones)
  • 8 red dates
  • 2 dried honey dates
  • 6-8 dried scallops — softened in a bowl of hot water for about 10-15 minutes
  • 1 dried cuttlefish — rinsed (optional)
  • 1.8l water
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Blanch pork ribs in a small pot of boiling water for about 5 minutes.
  2. Remove pork ribs from the pot and set aside.
  3. Put in all the ingredients in a soup pot.
  4. Bring to a boil and let it simmer on the lowest heat for 2 hours or until the meat and beans are soft.
  5. Season with salt to taste and serve hot.
