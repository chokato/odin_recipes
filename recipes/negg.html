Negg Soup

A light and zesty favorite straight from the Soup Faerie’s cookbook, Negg Soup is as bright and cheerful as a sunny day in Neopia. With the delicate sweetness of fresh Neggs and a hint of garden herbs, this soup is perfect for energizing tired Neopians after a day of adventuring. Simple, nourishing, and utterly magical!

Ingredients

  • 4 Fresh Neggs (any variety of choice), peeled and diced
  • 3 cups Crystal Clear Spring Water
  • 1 cup Golden Neggnog
  • 1 small Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 sticks of Celery, diced
  • 1 teaspoon Everdew Herbs
  • A dash of Snowflake Pepper
  • A sprinkle of Moonlit Salt

Steps

  1. In a cauldron-like pot, sauté the onion and celery in a splash of Crystal Clear Spring Water until softened and fragrant.
  2. Add the diced Neggs, stirring gently as their sweetness begins to bloom.
  3. Pour in the remaining Crystal Clear Spring Water, and bring to a cheerful simmer. Add Everdew Herbs, Snowflake Pepper, and Moonlit Salt to taste.
  4. Once the Neggs are tender and their magic has infused the broth, stir in the Golden Neggnog to give the soup a creamy, luxurious texture.
  5. Garnish with a sprig of Everdew Herbs for extra charm.
